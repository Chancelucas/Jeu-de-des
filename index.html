RULES

  1. The game is played by two players taking turns.
  2. Each player starts with a score of zero.
  3. On each turn, a player rolls a six-sided die.
  4. The number rolled on the die is added to the player's current score.
  5. If the player rolls a 1, their current score becomes zero, and their turn ends.
  6. The player can choose to "Hold" at any time during their turn to keep their current score and end their turn.
  7. When a player chooses to "Hold", their current score is added to their total score.
  8. The first player to reach or exceed a predetermined winning score wins the game.
  9. If a player reaches the winning score, the other player gets one final turn to try and surpass their score.
  10. The player with the highest total score at the end of the game is the winner.
